Fashion Villa:
Description:
Fashion Villa is an app where you find the best way to adopt the latest fashion, here are some most important categories, brands, and important tips for Fashion.

Features:
•	Fully research based application on Adoption of Fashion.
•	The Research results on the How to choose best fashion which suits your personality.
•	Fully Different User Interface Designed keeping in focus point of view of Niche (Target) Market.
•	 Live Tiles(Locally & Remotely)
•	Searching feature.
•	Fully Unique Branding (Unique Logo Designing & Graphics Designing).
